# User Stories

Our team has identified the following relevant user stories for our Market. Each user story is organized by business process.

### Stories related to *Tutoring Sessions*

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to automatically match available students requesting help in a subject specific to my expertise, *so that* I can provide a profound and in depth tutoring session for my students.

1. As a **student**, *I want* to schedule a tutoring session on the weekend *so that* it does not conflict with my school schedule, and my personal life

1. As a **student**, *I want* to let tutors know my preferred tutoring session lengths *so that* I don't burn out during a session.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to sit in on my child's tutoring session remotely, *so that* I can ensure the tutor is providing a quality service, and that my child is cooperating and behaving properly.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to schedule a tutoring session for my child, *so that* I can avoid conflicts with my child's school schedule, and my personal life, as well as ensure my child is scheduling tutoring sessions.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to schedule a tutoring session for my child, *so that* I can avoid conflicts with my child's school schedule, and my personal life, as well as ensure my child is scheduling tutoring sessions.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to setup autopay for my child's tutoring either by the session, or on a monthly basis, *so that* I can avoid the hassle of manually paying for each session.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to automatically match available students requesting help in a subject specific to my expertise, *so that* I can provide a profound and in depth tutoring session for my students.

### Stories related to *Tutor Qualifications*

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to be able to verify a tutor's academic and professional qualifications, *so that* I can ensure that the tutor is qualified to teach the subject they are offering.

1. As a **admin**, *I want* want to be able to be notified when a tutor's police information expires, *so that* I can ensure that tutors are constantly vetted at all times, and will not pose a potential danger to our students.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able upload any necessary documentation and verification directly on the mobile application during the sign up process, *so that* I can handle everything on one device.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to upload my academic and professional qualifications directly on the mobile application during the sign up process, *so that* I can handle everything on one device.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to import my calendar into the application to setup my availability and further edit that availability in the application, *so that* I can easily manage my tutoring schedule with my other calendars.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to able to go online during times outside my schedule and get matched with students, *so that* I can offer tutoring during my free time.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to temporarily pause my account, *so that* I can take a break from tutoring without losing my account details, and tutoring history.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to see the verification status of academic and background checks for any tutor at all times, *so that* I can know that my child is getting safe and quality tutoring when I am not there to supervise.

### Stories related to *Matching & Scheduling* and *Minimum Weekly Sessions*

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to be able to manually assign student to a tutoring session, *so that* I can fulfill custom requests from parents, or step in if the automatic matching system is not performing optimally.

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to be observe the performance of the automated scheduler through a variety of measures, *so that* I may know whether we must contact the developers to fine tune the scheduling algorithm.

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to ensure that our tutors are all receiving a minimum of 1 tutoring sessions per week, *so that* we can retain our tutors with our company.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be auto-matched with students who do not have a history of payment issues, so that my tutoring sessions are compensated.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to have my schedule assigned every week, *so that* I can plan my week accordingly.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to cancel a tutoring session, *so that* I may respond to unforeseen circumstances.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to cancel a tutoring session if no one shows up after 15 minutes and still get paid, *so that* my lost time is compensated.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to add and view past notes from other tutors that describe the student learning habits and styles, their weaknesses and strengths so that I can best tailor my teaching styles and avoid potential conflicts.

1. As a **student**, *I want* to be able to see a tutors reviews and ratings, *so that* I can avoid a potentially bad experience.

1. As a **student**, *I want* to be able to cancel a session, *so that* I may respond to unforeseen circumstances.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to upload my child's school schedule, *so that* the auto scheduler can best match my child with a tutor.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to view my child's attendance and cancelation history, *so that* I can ensure my child is attending their scheduled tutoring sessions and that I am not being charged for missed sessions.

### Stories related to *Communications and Updates*

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to be able to send out email notifications to all tutors, parents, and tutors, *so that* I can inform them of important information and updates.

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to be able to forcefully log out a user, *so that* I can verify the intended user is the one using the account.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to message administration through through the app, and have a history of all messages, *so that* I can easily track my communications with the administration.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to send a private message to parents, *so that* I can communicate with them about their child tutoring sessions.

1. As a **tutor or student**, *I want* to be able to send message to students, *so that* I can communicate with them during, before, or after their tutoring sessions and reasonably answer any questions they may have about what they learned.

1. As a **student**, *I want* to be able to message my tutor, *so that* I can ask questions about the material we are covering.

1. As a **student**, *I want* to be able to get notifications when my session is approaching or if there is a change, *so that* I can be reminded if I forget.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to supervise all communications between my child and the tutor, *so that* I can ensure that my child is receiving a quality tutoring session.

1. As a **parent**, *I want* to be able to receive updates of my child's progress and payment history through email if I choose, *so that* If i do not have the option of downloading the app, I can still be informed of my child's progress.

### Stories related to *General Usability*

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to track all transactions to ensure proper financial reporting and dispute resolution, *so that* I can ensure proper financial reporting and dispute resolution

1. As a **admin**, *I want* ensure the application is user friendly and complies to accessibility standards, *so that* I can ensure that all users can use the application.

1. As a **admin**, *I want* to ensure no illegal or inappropriate content or messages is being processed or stored through our service, *so that* I can ensure that our service is safe and legal.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to be able to view reports of my past earnings, *so that* I can track my finances.

### Stories that are potentially *Novel and Unique*

1. As a **tutor or student**, I want to have the ability to have live translation between the tutor and student, *so that* I can tutor students who speak a different language.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to have the ability to use a digital whiteboard, *so that* I can better explain concepts to students who prefer to learn visually.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to have the typeset mathematically in messages and questions, *so that* I can be more clear when responding to out-of-session questions that students may have.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to have the ability to upload 3d objects that students may interact with on app *so that* I can better explain concepts to students who prefer to learn visually.

1. As a **tutor**, *I want* to have the ability to upload notes to my sessions, *so that* I can provide students with offline resources to study from.

1. As a **tutee and student**, *I want* to have the ability chat with a LLM that is specifically trained on our tutoring sessions, using my uploaded notes as a primary knowledge base, *so that* I can provide students with offline interactive resources to study from.
